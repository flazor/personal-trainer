# Next Workout: Full Body (Bench Focus) — Deload

**This is a deload session.** Week 9 of the plan. Weights ~50% of last working sets, sets reduced to 2. Goal is recovery and crisp movement quality, not stimulus. After Friday's Day 2 deload, the following Tuesday (2026-05-05) is your bench 1RM test — protect that test by keeping today truly easy.

## Progression Notes
- **All weights ~50% of recent working loads, sets cut from 3-4 down to 2.** This is deliberate — deload weeks reset CNS fatigue and joints before testing. Do not push.
- **Bench 30 kg**: should feel very light. If it doesn't, your last working set was too hard or you're under-recovered — either way, hold the line.
- **Trap Bar DL 30 kg**: you've skipped this twice in a row due to time. Today it's a movement refresher, not a test. If pressed for time, do not skip — drop a warm-up exercise instead.
- **Hip Thrust bodyweight**: same story — you've skipped these. Bodyweight reset, then we'll re-load post-deload.
- **Friday 2026-05-01**: Day 2 deload (squat-focus, also 50% / 2 sets).
- **Tuesday 2026-05-05**: Bench 1RM test. Current 1RM = 70 kg (Jan 2026). Realistic target after 8 weeks of progression: 80-85 kg.
