# Next Workout: Full Body (Squat & Pull Focus) — Deload

**This is the second and final deload session.** Week 9, Day 2. Weights ~50% of Week 8 working sets, sets cut to 2. Goal is to arrive at Tuesday's 1RM test fully recovered — do not push, do not improvise heavier weights. After this, rest Wednesday and Thursday, then test your bench 1RM on **Tuesday 2026-05-05**.

## Warm-Up (10 min)
- 5 min light rowing or skierg (easy pace)
- Couch stretch: 60s per side
- Glute bridges: 2 x 10 (bodyweight, squeeze at top)
- Leg swings (front/back and lateral): 10 each direction per leg
- Bodyweight squats: 2 x 10
- Squat warm-up: 10 x 20 kg bar only

## Progression Notes
- **All weights ~50% of Week 8 loads, sets reduced to 2.** This is deliberate — CNS and joint recovery before the 1RM test.
- **Squat 27.5 kg**: should feel like a warm-up. If the bar feels heavy, that's a signal you're under-recovered — keep it easy regardless.
- **Lunges bodyweight**: you've been at 7.5–8 kg recently, so this is a true reset. Use it as a single-leg stability drill.
- **Face Pulls / Dead Bug**: consistently short on time in recent sessions — both are quick and important for shoulder health and APT. Do these, don't skip them.
- **Next session (Tue 2026-05-05)**: bench 1RM test. Current 1RM: 70 kg (Jan 2026). After 8 weeks of consistent 2.5 kg/week progression to 52.5 kg working sets of 5, a realistic target is **80–85 kg**. Rest Wednesday and Thursday to arrive fresh.

- Hamstring stretch (standing or lying): 60s per side
- Cat-cow: 10 slow reps
