# Next Workout: Speed + Lower Body Power

## Warm-Up (8–10 min)
- Light jog or skip: 3 min
- A-skips: 2 × 20 m
- High knees: 2 × 20 m
- Leg swings (forward/back, then side/side): 10 each leg
- Hip circles: 10 each direction
- Build-up sprints: 3 × 30 m at 60%, 75%, 85%

## Sprint Work (full recovery between reps)

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| 10 m Sprint (standing start) | 4 | 1 | Walk back + 60s | Focus on first-step explosion: low start, drive hard, 45° body lean. No 50m sprints yet — build the drive phase first |
| 30 m Sprint | 3 | 1 | Walk back + 90s | Smooth transition from drive to upright running. Stay relaxed in shoulders and face |

## Power & Strength

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Weight | Rest | Notes |
|----------|------|------|--------|------|-------|
| Box Jump (40 cm) | 3 | 5 | BW | 90s | Step down between reps, fully reset. Land soft — absorb with hips and knees. Est. starting height |
| Trap Bar Deadlift | 3 | 5 | 40 kg | 2 min | Pull the bar fast off the floor, control the lowering. Est. starting weight |
| Lateral Bound (stick) | 3 | 5 each | BW | 90s | Bound sideways, stick the landing for 2 sec. Quality over distance |
| Bulgarian Split Squat | 3 | 8 each | 8 kg DBs | 90s | Rear foot on bench, front shin vertical. Controlled down, drive up. Est. starting weight |

## Core

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Weight | Rest | Notes |
|----------|------|------|--------|------|-------|
| Pallof Press | 3 | 10 each side | Light cable | 60s | Stand tall, press out, resist rotation. Est. starting weight — use lightest setting that provides real resistance |

## Progression Notes
- All weights are estimated starting points — no prior logs exist. Focus on learning the movements and dialing in appropriate loads this week.
- Sprint work is technique-focused: cue "push the ground away behind you" on the 10 m starts.
- Box jumps: start at 40 cm. If landing is clean and confident, move to 50 cm next session.
- Trap bar deadlift: 40 kg should feel moderate. If all 5 reps are fast and clean, increase to 45 kg next Monday.

## Cooldown (5 min)
- Hip flexor stretch (kneeling lunge): 30s each side
- Hamstring stretch (standing): 30s each side
- Calf stretch (wall lean): 30s each side
- Quad stretch (standing): 30s each side
