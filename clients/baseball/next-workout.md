# Next Workout: Full Body A

## Warm-Up (5–8 min)
- 3–5 min easy row or bike
- Leg swings (forward/back, then side/side): 10 each leg
- Hip circles: 10 each direction
- Bodyweight squat: 10 slow reps — focus on depth and upright chest
- Glute bridge: 10 reps

## Session

| Exercise               | Sets | Reps         | Weight     | Rest | Notes                                                                        |
| ---------------------- | ---- | ------------ | ---------- | ---- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Goblet Squat           | 3    | 10–12        | 16 kg KB   | 90s  | Est. starting weight — hold KB at chest, sit tall, heels down                |

## Progression Notes
- All weights are estimated starting points — this is your first gym session.
- These loads should feel manageable (RPE 6–7). That's intentional — Week 1 is about learning the movements.
- If all reps on any exercise felt easy and form was clean, bump the weight by 2 kg next session.
- If form on any exercise felt shaky, keep the same weight next time and focus on the technique cue in the Notes column.
- After the session, log your actual weights and any notes (what felt hard, what felt easy).

## Cooldown (5 min)
- Hip flexor stretch (kneeling lunge): 30s each side
- Hamstring stretch (seated or standing): 30s each side
- Chest/pec stretch (doorframe or hands clasped behind back): 30s
